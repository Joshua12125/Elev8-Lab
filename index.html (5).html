FREQUENTLY ASKED

Questions researchers actually ask.

Are these products for human consumption?+
No. Every product sold by Elev-8 Labs is strictly for laboratory research use. Nothing we sell is a drug, supplement, or medical product, and nothing is intended for human or veterinary use, consumption, or administration in any form. Purchasing requires attestation that the compound will be used solely for in-vitro research by a qualified buyer.
Is every lot tested before it ships?+
Yes. Every lot is verified by an accredited third-party laboratory using HPLC and mass spectrometry before it's cleared to ship — we don't rely on self-certification or periodic sampling.
Can I get a Certificate of Analysis?+
Yes. A COA is available for every lot number, matched to the specific lot you receive. Request one through the contact form or by emailing support directly.
How is my order shipped and stored?+
Orders are dispatched within 1–3 business days in insulated, cold-pack mailers to preserve compound integrity in transit. On arrival, store according to the handling guidance included with your COA.
Who can purchase from Elev-8 Labs?+
Access is limited to verified researchers and institutions purchasing for laboratory or in-vitro research purposes. We may request credentials or institutional affiliation before fulfilling an order.
What if I have a question about a specific lot?+
Reach our support team with the lot number from your order or COA — staff with laboratory backgrounds can walk through testing methodology and results directly.
