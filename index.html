Verification Process

Every Batch. Every Step.
No Exceptions.

We don't rely on supplier assurances. Every product goes through a documented multi-step verification process before it ever ships.

01
Controlled Sourcing

We source exclusively from vetted synthesis partners with documented quality management systems. No intermediary brokers. Direct, traceable supplier relationships only.

02
HPLC Purity Analysis

High-Performance Liquid Chromatography confirms purity at 99%+. Results reported as actual numerical values — not pass/fail statements. Every batch, every time.

03
Mass Spectrometry Identity

Mass spectrometry independently confirms the molecular identity of each product, ensuring complete accuracy before any batch is approved for dispatch.

04
COA Issued & Filed

Full Certificate of Analysis compiled with lot number, test date, testing laboratory, purity %, and identity confirmation. Available on request with every order.

05
Cold-Pack Dispatch

Verified batches are lyophilized, sealed, and packed in insulated cold-pack mailers. Dispatched via USPS or UPS Priority within 1–3 business days of payment confirmation.

Frequently Asked

Questions We Get
Most Often

Everything you need to know about documentation, shipping, and our policies — answered directly and completely.

Direct Support
support@elev-8labs.com
Do you provide a Certificate of Analysis?▾
Yes. Every product is sold with a Certificate of Analysis available on request. The COA documents purity (HPLC), identity confirmation (mass spectrometry), lot number, test date, and the testing laboratory. Email support@elev-8labs.com with your order number to request the COA for any order.
How are products shipped and stored?▾
Products are shipped from our Florida facility via USPS or UPS Priority within 1–3 business days of payment confirmation. All temperature-sensitive items ship inside insulated cold-pack mailers. Lyophilized vials should be stored at –20°C for long-term storage.
What payment methods do you accept?▾
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, Apple Pay and supported digital wallets. All orders must be paid in full before shipment. Checkout is SSL-encrypted and fully secured.
Do you ship internationally?▾
We currently ship within the United States only. Email support@elev-8labs.com to be notified when international shipping becomes available for your country.
What is your refund and replacement policy?▾
We replace or refund any shipment that arrives damaged, incorrect, or with a documentation discrepancy. Notify us within 7 days of delivery at support@elev-8labs.com with your order number and a photo of the issue.
How do I reach support for technical questions?▾
Email support@elev-8labs.com directly. Our team has laboratory backgrounds and answers technical questions substantively — not with scripted responses. You can also reach us through the contact form on elev-8labs.com.
